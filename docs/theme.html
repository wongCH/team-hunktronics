6 minimal theme mockups

Each card renders a live mini-preview of the real app shell — nav rail, conversation list, chat stream and composer — so you can judge every palette in context, not as flat swatches. The collection covers dark and light design systems with reduced chroma, one confident accent, generous negative space, hairline borders and soft focus glows.

Graphite Amber01 · neutral warm

Truly neutral slate greys (no blue cast) with a warm amber accent. Feels like premium hardware UI; the warm signal reads well for actions and highlights.

Indigo Nebula02 · gradient dark

A soft indigo accent floating over a violet nebula gradient. Deep, premium and modern — the gradient adds quiet depth without breaking the minimalist calm.

Neon Green Grid03 · gradient dark

Vivid signal-green over a deep emerald gradient. High-energy and terminal-like — the glowing accent reads as “live” and active against the near-black canvas.

Graphite Blue04 · focused dark

Deep graphite layers with a crisp cyan-blue accent. Keeps the dark, technical character while making primary actions and active states immediately clear.

Linear Indigo Light05 · productivity light

Clean white layers, quiet violet-grey separation and a focused indigo accent. Preserves the Linear-inspired productivity feel for bright environments.

Graphite Light06 · neutral light

Cool graphite-tinted surfaces with a restrained blue accent. The layered neutrals reduce glare while keeping tables, navigation and controls highly legible.

Recommendation. Use 04 Graphite Blue for a focused technical dark theme, 05 Linear Indigo Light for a clean productivity theme, or 06 Graphite Light for the calmest bright workspace. The original five remain available for warmer, neon and gradient directions.
